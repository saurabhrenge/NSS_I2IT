NOT ME BUT YOU

At NSS, we believe in the power of youth to bring positive change. Our unit actively engages in community development, social awareness drives, and volunteering activities that foster leadership, compassion, and a strong sense of social responsibility. From blood donation camps and cleanliness drives to digital literacy and rural outreach programs, NSS volunteers work with passion and purpose to serve society. Join us in our mission to learn, serve, and grow—because true education goes beyond the classroom.
